Controls

Use the up arrow to move forward.

Use the down arrow to move back.

Use the left arrow to move left.

Use the right arrow to move right.

Use the spacebar to interact with objects in the room.

Instructions

The purpose of this game is to gather the objects, solve the puzzle and escape the room as quickly as you can.

Walk around the room and interact with objects to find the clues that will help you solve the puzzle.

Once you solve the puzzle, you will understand the secret of the room and be able to escape.
