Instructions

Background Story

Grace has been kidnapped in her sleep and locked in an unknown room.

Gather the clues to discover who the culprit is and escape the room!

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move forward, back, left and right.

Use the spacebar to interact with objects in the room.

Instructions

The purpose of this game is to gather the objects, solve the puzzle and escape the room as quickly as you can.

Walk around the room and interact with objects to find the clues that will help you solve the puzzle.

If you want to quit the game or restart, press esc. Move to resume the game.
